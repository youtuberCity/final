Who are you?

Hey there!

Oh, didn't see you there. We're YouTuber.City, a startup business dedicated to providing the best user experience possible. Founded by Jonah Cohen and Jared Pawlyzyn in July 2017, we've always striven to provide the best content possible. YouTuber.City is the start of a new era on the web, and we're glad to have been the ones that created it.

Isn't this illegal?

Nope! YouTuber.City is 100% legal to use and operate. You won't get in any trouble through using YouTuber.City. We don't provide any personal information (addresses, phone numbers, exact location, etc.) so we can keep YouTubers safe. If you're listed on our site and have a problem, please contact us.

This is awesome! Who made it?

We're glad you asked. YouTuber.City was created by Jonah Cohen while he was watching YouTube videos. He thought "I wonder if I'm close to any big YouTubers..." and searched online for a tracker, finding nothing. He passed the idea along to his programmer friend, Jared Pawlyzyn, and the pair got to work. Within two days, YouTuber.City Version 1.0.0 was launched! The rest is history!

Someone's Missing...

If you want to see someone on our tracker and they're missing, simply contact us! Please note that the minimum to get added to our database is 100,000 subscribers, with case-by-case exceptions for unique YouTubers and businesses. 

How do I use it? 

YouTuber.City is extremely easy to use. Simply input your location inside of the search bar on the tracker, select a radius, and press the search button! Doing this will pull up all of the YouTubers within the radius that you selected. Simply click on one of the pins to see who it is! Just one quick thing, the tracker isn't yet officially supported on mobile devices. We're launching an app soon. Sorry about that.
