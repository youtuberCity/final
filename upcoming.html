So, what happens next?

YouTuber.City is a brand new company, so it only makes sense that we're developing new products. If we're ever working on something, it'll be listed below.

Live Location Tracker

In the next few months, we'll be rolling out a new feature on select YouTubers that allows you to find the town a YouTuber is in at that very moment. We're calling this "YouTuber City Live" and we're very excited to launch it.

If you want to help test this feature, please email us at inquiries@youtuber.city

Google Play Store App

At some point in the future, we want to release an app on Google Play for all devices running Android to bring the ​convenience of YouTuber City to the pocket.
